Common Issues We Can Identify

Water Stress

Yellowing leaves or dry soil may indicate insufficient watering.

Overwatering

Mold on the soil surface or root rot can be signs of overwatering.

Sunlight Deficiency

Pale or weak growth might suggest your bonsai needs more sunlight.
