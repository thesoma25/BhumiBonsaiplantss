Bonsai Care Guide

Indoor vs. Outdoor Bonsai Care

Indoor bonsai require consistent temperature and humidity, while outdoor bonsai need natural sunlight and protection from extreme weather. Choose the right environment for your bonsai's species.

Watering Schedules

Water your bonsai when the soil surface feels dry. The frequency depends on the species, pot size, and climate. Avoid overwatering, which can lead to root rot.

Pruning Basics

Pruning shapes your bonsai and encourages growth. Use sharp bonsai shears to trim new shoots and branches. Different pruning techniques apply to different bonsai styles.

Soil & Repotting Tips

Bonsai soil needs to drain well. Repot your bonsai every 2-5 years, depending on its growth rate. Use a bonsai-specific soil mix and gently untangle the roots during repotting.

Seasonal Maintenance

Adjust your bonsai care routine with the seasons. Provide more sunlight and water during the growing season (spring and summer) and protect your bonsai from frost in winter.
